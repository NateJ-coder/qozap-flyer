What is QoZap?

QoZap simplifies quoting, invoicing, and payments for service providers who work on-site. Built for mobile from day one, it's your all-in-one tool to run and grow your service business.

  • For plumbers, electricians, cleaners, handymen
  • For beauty professionals, tutors, pet groomers, more

Core Features

  • Send quotes instantly via WhatsApp, email or link
  • On-the-spot invoice generation after service
  • Dynamic QR code for real-time client payment
  • Secure payment options: EFT, card, PayPal (coming)
  • Automated reminders & payment tracking
  • Real-time price updates from stores like Buco & Midas

Community Hub

  • Showcase your services
  • Collaborate & connect with other professionals
  • Use the Client SOS system for referral jobs

Smart Pricing

  • Live material pricing from your preferred suppliers
  • Custom markups for accurate cost estimates
  • No more guesswork in quoting or billing
