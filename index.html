What is QoZap?

QoZap simplifies quoting, invoicing, and payments for service providers who work on-site. Built for mobile from day one, it's your all-in-one tool to run and grow your service business. The app is under active development with input from real professionals like you.

Who it’s for:

  • Plumbers, electricians, cleaners, handymen
  • Beauty and wellness professionals (e.g., hair, nails, massage)
  • Private tutors, pet groomers, personal trainers, and freelancers

Core Features

TL;DR: Instantly quote, invoice, and get paid—with reminders and real-time product pricing built in.

  • Create & share quotes instantly via WhatsApp, email, or SMS
  • One-tap invoice generation at job completion
  • Display a QR code to accept instant payments on-site
  • Secure client payment options (EFT, card, PayPal coming soon)
  • Live pricing on materials pulled from your local suppliers
  • Auto-reminders for unpaid invoices and instant payment logging
  • Store client preferences, previous jobs, and payment records (coming soon)

Community Hub

  • Upload your own service catalog for others to view
  • Exchange tips and advice in real-time forums
  • Use the Client SOS feature to refer clients or accept handover jobs

Smart Pricing

  • Auto-sync prices from vendors like Buco, Midas & more
  • Apply your own custom markup percentages
  • Generate material cost estimates for quotes and invoices instantly

FAQs

Is QoZap free?

We're finalizing pricing models that will offer great value. Early users may receive extended free access.

How does QR payment work?

You display the QR on your device. Clients scan it and pay on a secure web page. No app download needed.

Do I need internet access on-site?

Yes, to generate/send invoices and access pricing or payment tools, some connectivity is required.
