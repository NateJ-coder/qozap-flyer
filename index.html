What is QoZap?

QoZap simplifies quoting, invoicing, and payments for service providers who work on-site. Built for mobile from day one, it's your all-in-one tool to run and grow your service business.

Who it’s for:

  • Plumbers, electricians, cleaners, handymen
  • Beauty and wellness professionals
  • Private tutors, pet groomers, personal trainers

Core Features

TL;DR: Instantly quote, invoice, and get paid—with client reminders and real-time pricing built in.

  • Generate & share quotes instantly via WhatsApp or email
  • One-tap invoice generation after job completion
  • Dynamic QR codes for client payments
  • Secure online gateway: EFT, card, PayPal (future)
  • Real-time inventory pricing from suppliers (e.g., Buco, Midas)
  • Payment notifications & automatic reminders
  • Store client history & preferences (future)

Community Hub

  • Share and browse service catalogs
  • Engage in peer discussion forums
  • Use "Client SOS" to refer or pick up new jobs

Smart Pricing

  • Auto-updated supplier pricing for materials
  • Custom markup configurations during onboarding
  • Transparent on-site pricing = faster client approval

FAQs

Is QoZap free?

We’re testing pricing options to keep things affordable. You'll be informed before launch.

How does QR payment work?

Clients scan your device's code to pay on a secure web page—no app needed on their side.

Do I need internet on-site?

Yes, internet access is needed to send invoices and accept online payments.
