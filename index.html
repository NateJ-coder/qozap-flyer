Overview

What is QoZap?

Imagine a world where invoicing and getting paid on the go is seamless and stress-free. That's the vision behind QoZap! Currently under development in Port Alfred, our mobile-friendly app is being crafted to simplify the lives of service providers who are always on the move, working directly with clients at their homes or various service locations. We recognize the common frustrations of juggling paperwork, tracking payments, and the delays that can come with it. QoZap is our planned solution to bring efficiency and ease to your daily workflow, letting you concentrate on what you do best â€“ providing excellent service.

Core Principles (Planned)

  • Simplicity: We aim for an intuitive and user-friendly interface that anyone can navigate. Say goodbye to complex systems and hello to straightforward digital invoicing, once our development is complete.
  • Efficiency: QoZap is being engineered to help you create and send invoices quickly. Plus, with our planned integrated QR code payment system, getting paid faster is our goal.
  • Mobile-First: Designed with a mobile-first approach, we're ensuring a smooth and fully functional experience on your smartphone or tablet when the app launches.

Target Audience

Our initial focus is on empowering the vibrant community of service providers right here in Port Alfred. If you fall into any of these categories, QoZap is being designed with you in mind:

  • Home Heroes: Cleaning services, maintenance crews, and anyone who keeps our homes running smoothly.
  • Mobile beauty and wellness: Mobile hairdressers, nail technicians, massage therapists, and wellness practitioners who bring their expertise to clients.
  • Knowledge Sharers: Tutors, personal trainers, and instructors who provide one-on-one guidance.
  • Pet Pals: Dedicated pet groomers, dog walkers, and animal caregivers who are always on the move.
  • Handy Helpers: Electricians, plumbers, carpenters, and all-around handymen who tackle repairs and installations.

Key Features (Under Development)

  • Quote Generation & Sharing: After onboarding, service providers can generate quotes within the app and share them with clients in person or via WhatsApp/email.
  • On-Site Service & Invoice Preparation: Upon service completion, providers can tally expenses (e.g., materials), input their employee details, and tap "get invoice."
  • Automated Invoice Generation: The app will calculate total costs (expenses + labour + service fees based on onboarding setup) and generate a professional invoice with all the service provider's information, including the servicing employee's details and address. (Other compulsory professional invoice details will also be included).
  • Flexible Payment Options with QR Code & Sharing: A QR code will be generated on the provider's phone for immediate scanning by the client. Alternatively, the invoice can be shared with the client via email, WhatsApp, or other platforms.
  • Client-Side Payment Gateway: Clients will have a "pay now" button, redirecting them to a secure payment gateway to choose their preferred method: PayPal, other EFT gateways, card payment, or direct EFT (using the service provider's bank details provided during onboarding).
  • Automated Payment Reminders: Clients with outstanding bills will receive automated payment reminders (via email, WhatsApp, or the app, if downloaded) every 24 hours.
  • Real-Time Payment Tracking & Notifications: Service providers will receive instant notifications upon client payment, and the app will automatically log the payment, aiding in tracking outstanding invoices and payment history.
  • (Future Development) Enhanced Repeat Service: For returning clients, the app will store past service history. Upon payment for new services (via QR code or other methods), client payment details can be saved to streamline future transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of service providers will QoZap be for?

Initially, we're focusing on supporting a wide range of on-location service professionals in Port Alfred, including home cleaning and maintenance services, mobile beauty and wellness providers, tutors and personal trainers, pet groomers and walkers, as well as handymen and various repair services.

Will QoZap be free to use?

We're currently exploring the best pricing model to ensure QoZap offers significant value without breaking the bank for our local service providers. We're committed to providing a cost-effective solution, and we'll be sharing more details about our pricing structure as we get closer to the official launch.

How will clients pay through the QR code?

When we implement the QR code payment feature in QoZap, your client will simply use their smartphone's camera or a QR code scanning app to scan it. This will instantly direct them to a secure online payment page where they can conveniently complete the transaction using their preferred payment method. We're planning to integrate with popular and trusted payment gateways.

What will happen after a client pays?

Once a client successfully makes a payment through the QR code system (once implemented), you'll receive an immediate notification within the QoZap app. The invoice status will also be automatically updated to "Paid" in your records.

Will I be able to use QoZap on my phone and computer?

QoZap is being developed with a strong mobile-first approach. We're also exploring the possibility of offering web access for certain functionalities in the future.
